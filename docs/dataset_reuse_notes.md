# Dataset Reuse Notes

## Original Dataset Source

The data in this repository are derived from the following publication:

**Sarmiento L, Pérez-Almeida I, Díaz B, Álvarez H, Viera W.** Molecular marker-based characterization of Ecuadorian dry forest tamarind plus trees. *Bioagro*. 2017;29(3):153-162. Available from: [https://ve.scielo.org/scielo.php?script=sci_arttext&pid=S1316-33612017000300001](https://ve.scielo.org/scielo.php?script=sci_arttext&pid=S1316-33612017000300001). Accessed April 18, 2026.

## Nature of Reuse

- This dataset is a FAIR-compliant reprocessing of ISSR marker data originally presented in the article above.
- Elevation and geographical coordinates were extracted manually from the publication’s tabulated information.
- Binary matrices were derived and curated to allow reproducible computation of diversity metrics and multivariate analyses.
- No proprietary or protected data has been reused.
- The reused content is limited to publicly available tabular information presented in the figures and tables of the source article.

## Purpose

- The reuse is intended for digital preservation, reproducibility, and public access to the processed data under FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable) principles.
- This version enables further computational reuse by the scientific community, particularly for those studying genetic diversity, agroforestry, or conservation of *Tamarindus indica L.* in Ecuador.
